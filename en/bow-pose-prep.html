Bow Pose Prep

Bow Pose Prep is a powerful yoga posture that builds strength, flexibility, and body awareness. Regular practice develops balance between effort and ease.

Steps

  1. 1Begin in a stable, comfortable starting position
  2. 2Engage your core and establish a steady breath
  3. 3Move gradually into the full expression of this pose
  4. 4Align joints properly — knees over ankles, shoulders over wrists
  5. 5Hold for 3-5 breaths, then release with control

Tips

Focus on alignment over depth. Use props like blocks or straps to maintain proper form. Listen to your body and avoid forcing the pose.

FAQ

What are the main benefits of Bow Pose Prep?

Bow Pose Prep builds core strength, improves flexibility, and develops body awareness. It also promotes mental focus and stress reduction through mindful breathing.
