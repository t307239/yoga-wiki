Gorilla Pose

Category: Forward Bends Level: Intermediate

A standing forward fold where hands are tucked under the feet to deepen the hamstring stretch.

🇺🇸 English 🇯🇵 日本語 🇧🇷 Português

Benefits

  • Deeply stretches hamstrings
  • Warms the palms and wrists
  • Calms the nervous system
  • Improves circulation

How to Do It

  1. Stand with feet hip-width apart and fold forward
  2. Bend knees slightly to slide hands under feet, palms up
  3. Straighten legs as much as possible
  4. Let head hang heavy
  5. Hold for 5-10 breaths
