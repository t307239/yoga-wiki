Half Lotus Forward Bend

Seated Forward Fold

A meditative seated forward fold combining the hip-opening qualities of half lotus with a calming spinal extension, bridging flexibility and stillness.

Benefits

Opens hip rotators and outer hip, stretches hamstrings and spine, calms nervous system, prepares for full lotus, develops meditative focus

Instructions

Sit with legs extended. Bend right knee, placing right foot high on left thigh (half lotus). Flex left foot. Inhale to lengthen spine. Exhale, fold forward over the left leg, reaching for the foot. Hold, then switch sides.

Tips

Only attempt if knee is comfortable in half lotus. Use a strap around the foot if needed. Keep the folded knee supported on a blanket if it lifts.

Duration

Hold 1-3 minutes each side

Contraindications

Knee injuries, ankle injuries, tight hips
