Fire Hydrant Pose

Category: Hip Opening Level: Beginner

A hip-opening pose that strengthens the glutes and improves lateral hip mobility.

🇺🇸 English 🇯🇵 日本語 🇧🇷 Português

Benefits

  • Opens hips laterally
  • Strengthens glutes
  • Improves hip mobility
  • Relieves lower back tension

How to Do It

  1. Start on all fours in tabletop position
  2. Keep your right knee bent at 90 degrees
  3. Lift your right knee out to the side, like a dog at a fire hydrant
  4. Keep your hips level and core engaged
  5. Hold, then slowly lower and repeat on the other side
