Standing Pigeon Pose

Category: Standing Level: Intermediate

A standing balance pose that deeply opens the hip and stretches the piriformis muscle.

🇺🇸 English 🇯🇵 日本語 🇧🇷 Português

Benefits

  • Deep hip opener
  • Improves balance
  • Stretches piriformis
  • Strengthens standing leg

How to Do It

  1. Stand on your left leg and bring right ankle to left thigh
  2. Flex the right foot to protect the knee
  3. Bend the standing leg slightly and hinge forward at hips
  4. Bring hands to prayer or floor for balance
  5. Hold for 5-10 breaths then switch sides
