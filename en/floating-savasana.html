Floating Savasana Variation

Category: Restorative Level: Beginner

An elevated savasana variation using props under knees for deep lower back release.

🇺🇸 English 🇯🇵 日本語 🇧🇷 Português

Benefits

  • Relieves lower back pressure
  • Allows deeper relaxation
  • Reduces spinal tension
  • Supports natural lumbar curve

How to Do It

  1. Place a bolster or rolled blanket under knees
  2. Lie back and let legs rest on support
  3. Arms 45 degrees from body, palms up
  4. Close eyes and breathe naturally
  5. Stay for 5-10 minutes in complete stillness
