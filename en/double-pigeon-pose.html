Double Pigeon Pose

Sanskrit: Agnisthasana Double
Difficulty: Intermediate
Category: Hip Opener

Benefits

Intense double hip opener. Opens glutes and hip external rotators deeply.

Steps

  1. Sit with left leg extended
  2. Stack right shin over left knee
  3. Keep right leg there
  4. Extend left leg long
  5. Both shins stacked
  6. Fold forward
  7. Hold 1–3 minutes

Tips

This is more intense than single-leg versions.

FAQ

Should I feel this in both hips?
Yes, that is the point — opening both sides of the hip simultaneously.
