Supported Side Stretch

Supported Side Stretch is a rewarding yoga posture that cultivates strength, flexibility, and mindful awareness. Regular practice helps integrate body and breath for a balanced state of being.

Steps

  1. 1Begin in a stable, grounded starting position and establish a calm breath
  2. 2Activate your core gently and find your natural spinal alignment
  3. 3Move gradually and intentionally into the shape of Supported Side Stretch
  4. 4Align key joints — check knees, hips, shoulders and engage supporting muscles
  5. 5Hold for 3-8 breaths, then release slowly with full control

Tips

Prioritize alignment over depth in Supported Side Stretch. Props like blocks, blankets, or straps can make the pose accessible and safe. Breathe steadily throughout — if the breath becomes labored, ease back slightly.

FAQ

Who should avoid Supported Side Stretch?

Those with relevant joint injuries or acute inflammation should modify or skip Supported Side Stretch. Always consult a qualified yoga teacher or healthcare professional if you have any concerns.
