Upward Dog

Category: Backbend Level: Beginner Sanskrit: Urdhva Mukha Svanasana

Step-by-Step Guide

1
Lie face down, hands by lower ribs
2
Press tops of feet into mat
3
Inhale and straighten arms, lifting chest and thighs off mat
4
Roll shoulder blades back and down
5
Thighs and tops of feet are the only contact points
6
Hold 1–3 breaths

Benefits

Opens chest and stretches front body. Strengthens arms and spine. Used extensively in Sun Salutations.

Pro Tips

If holding the full version is too intense, keep thighs on the mat and practice Cobra Pose instead.

FAQ

Q: What's the difference between Upward Dog and Cobra Pose?

In Upward Dog the thighs lift completely off the mat. In Cobra the pelvis remains grounded. Upward Dog is more intense and requires more arm strength.
