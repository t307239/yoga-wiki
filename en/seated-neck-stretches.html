Seated Neck Stretches

Category: Seated Level: Beginner

A gentle sequence of neck stretches to relieve tension in the cervical spine and trapezius.

🇺🇸 English 🇯🇵 日本語 🇧🇷 Português

Benefits

  • Relieves neck tension
  • Reduces headaches
  • Improves cervical mobility
  • Counters desk posture

How to Do It

  1. Sit tall with shoulders relaxed
  2. Drop right ear toward right shoulder
  3. Optionally place right hand on head for gentle weight
  4. Hold for 3-5 breaths then switch sides
  5. End with chin tucks and head circles
