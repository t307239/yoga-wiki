Reverse Warrior Pose

Category: Standing Level: Beginner

A graceful side stretch from warrior II that opens the side body and improves flexibility.

🇺🇸 English 🇯🇵 日本語 🇧🇷 Português

Benefits

  • Stretches side body
  • Strengthens legs
  • Opens chest
  • Improves lateral flexibility

How to Do It

  1. Begin in Warrior II with right foot forward
  2. Flip front palm to face up
  3. Inhale and reach front arm up and back
  4. Back hand slides down the back leg
  5. Gaze up toward ceiling or back hand
