Mermaid Pose

Mermaid Pose is a rewarding yoga posture that cultivates strength, flexibility, and mindful awareness. Regular practice helps integrate body and breath for a balanced state of being.

Steps

  1. 1Begin in a stable, grounded starting position and establish a calm breath
  2. 2Activate your core gently and find your natural spinal alignment
  3. 3Move gradually and intentionally into the full expression of this pose
  4. 4Align key joints — check knees, hips, shoulders and engage supporting muscles
  5. 5Hold for 3-8 breaths, then release slowly with full control

Tips

Prioritize alignment over depth in Mermaid Pose. Props like blocks, blankets, or straps can make the pose accessible and safe. Breathe steadily throughout — if the breath becomes labored, ease back slightly.

FAQ

Who should avoid Mermaid Pose?

Those with relevant joint injuries or acute inflammation should modify or skip Mermaid Pose. Always consult a qualified yoga teacher or healthcare professional if you have any concerns.
