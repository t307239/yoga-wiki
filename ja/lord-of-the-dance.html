Lord of the Dance

カテゴリ: バランス レベル: 上級 サンスクリット名: Natarajasana

ステップバイステップガイド

1
Stand in Mountain Pose, shift weight to left foot
2
Bend right knee and grasp right ankle from inside with right hand
3
Kick right foot into hand, lifting leg behind you
4
Extend left arm forward, parallel to floor
5
Keep torso upright as leg rises
6
Hold 5 breaths and repeat other side

効果・メリット

Stunning backbend and balance pose. Opens shoulders, chest and hip flexors. Builds focus and poise.

プロのアドバイス

Use a wall or chair for balance support. Open shoulders with Eagle arms before attempting the full expression.

よくある質問

Q: How do I avoid this pose straining my lower back?

Engage your core and press your pelvis forward. Think of lifting the back leg by kicking into your hand rather than collapsing the lower back.
