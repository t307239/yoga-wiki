Supported Fish Pose

Sânscrito: Matsyasana Supported
Dificuldade: Beginner
Categoria: Backbend

Benefícios

Gentle backbend that opens chest and throat. Restorative and calming.

Passos

  1. Place block horizontally under upper back
  2. Set block at medium height
  3. Lie back over block
  4. Arms extended or hands on chest
  5. Let head rest lightly on block
  6. Hold 1–3 minutes

Dicas

Use a bolster if a block feels too intense.

Perguntas Frequentes

How long can I stay in Supported Fish?
30 seconds to 3 minutes depending on comfort. This is gentle enough for extended holds.
