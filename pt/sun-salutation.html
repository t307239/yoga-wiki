Sun Salutation

Categoria: Sequência Nível: Iniciante Nome Sânscrito: Surya Namaskar

Guia Passo a Passo

1
Mountain Pose — stand tall at front of mat
2
Raised Arms — inhale, sweep arms overhead
3
Forward Fold — exhale, fold at hips
4
Half Lift — inhale, lengthen spine, fingertips to shins
5
Plank to Chaturanga — exhale, step or jump back, lower slowly
6
Upward Dog — inhale, press up, open chest
7
Downward Dog — exhale, hips high, 5 breaths
8
Walk/jump feet forward, half lift, fold, rise to Mountain

Benefícios

The complete warm-up and sun salutation sequence. Builds heat, integrates the body, and links movement with breath.

Dicas Profissionais

Move at your own pace initially. Synchronize each movement precisely with one breath. Start with just 3 rounds.

FAQ

Q: How many Sun Salutations should I do per day?

Beginners: 3–5 rounds. Intermediate: 6–12 rounds. Advanced practitioners may do 108 on special occasions. Quality over quantity — focus on breath synchronization.
