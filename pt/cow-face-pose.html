Cow Face Pose

Categoria: Abertura de Quadril Nível: Intermediário Nome Sânscrito: Gomukhasana

Guia Passo a Passo

1
Sit and cross right knee over left knee, stacking knees
2
Flex both feet and sit evenly between heels
3
Raise right arm, bend elbow behind head
4
Bring left arm behind back, reach up toward right hand
5
Clasp hands or use a strap
6
Hold 1 minute and repeat other side

Benefícios

Intense hip and shoulder opener. Targets outer hips (piriformis) and shoulder rotators.

Dicas Profissionais

Use a strap between hands if they don't meet. Sit on a blanket to make hip stacking easier.

FAQ

Q: Why does Cow Face Pose hurt so much?

It targets the piriformis and hip rotators — areas that are chronically tight in most people. Go slowly, use props, and the sensation should be intense but not sharp or painful.
